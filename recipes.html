Simple Pasta
⏱️ Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | 👥 Serves: 2
📝 Ingredients:
  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional)
👨‍🍳 Instructions:
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta and cook according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  4. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss to combine.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot, optionally topped with fresh parsley.
Chicken Stir Fry
⏱️ Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | 👥 Serves: 4
📝 Ingredients:
  • 300g chicken breast, sliced thin
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
👨‍🍳 Instructions:
  1. Cut chicken into thin, bite-sized pieces.
  2. Chop vegetables into uniform sizes.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a wok over high heat.
  4. Add chicken and cook until golden and cooked through.
  5. Add sesame oil, garlic and ginger, cooking for 30 seconds.
  6. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Return chicken and add soy sauce.
  8. Toss everything and serve hot over rice.
Vegetable Salad
⏱️ Prep Time: 10 minutes | No cooking | 👥 Serves: 4
📝 Ingredients:
  • 2 cups lettuce, chopped
  • 1 large tomato, diced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
👨‍🍳 Instructions:
  1. Wash and chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Place lettuce in a large salad bowl.
  3. Layer tomatoes, cucumber, and onion on top.
  4. Whisk together olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  5. Drizzle dressing over salad.
  6. Toss gently to coat evenly.
  7. Season and serve immediately.
Chocolate Cake
⏱️ Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | 👥 Serves: 8
📝 Ingredients:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
👨‍🍳 Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 175°C and grease a 9-inch cake pan.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes until smooth.
  5. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Cool and demolded onto a wire rack.
Fish and Chips
⏱️ Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | 👥 Serves: 2
📝 Ingredients:
  • 400g white fish fillets
  • 4 large potatoes
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon wedges
👨‍🍳 Instructions:
  1. Cut potatoes into thick chips and soak in cold water for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a deep fryer to 175°C.
  3. Drain potatoes and fry until golden. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  4. Pat fish dry and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Beat egg and dip fish, then coat with flour.
  6. Fry fish until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve with chips and lemon wedges.
Veggie Burger
⏱️ Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | 👥 Serves: 4
📝 Ingredients:
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Burger buns and toppings
👨‍🍳 Instructions:
  1. Mash beans in a large bowl.
  2. Mix in oats, onion, pepper, garlic, egg, and cumin.
  3. Form mixture into patties.
  4. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Grill or pan-fry patties 4-5 minutes per side until golden.
  6. Serve on buns with your favorite toppings.
Macaroni & Cheese
⏱️ Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | 👥 Serves: 4
📝 Ingredients:
  • 200g macaroni pasta
  • 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tsp mustard powder
👨‍🍳 Instructions:
  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Whisk in flour to create a roux and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Gradually add milk, whisking constantly until smooth.
  5. Heat until sauce thickens, about 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in cheese and mustard powder until melted.
  7. Combine pasta with cheese sauce and season.
  8. Serve immediately while hot.
Cherry Pie
⏱️ Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | 👥 Serves: 8
📝 Ingredients:
  • 2 cups fresh cherries, pitted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp almond extract
  • Pre-made pie crust
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • 1 tbsp water
👨‍🍳 Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 190°C.
  2. Mix cherries, sugar, cornstarch, and almond extract.
  3. Line a pie dish with one crust and add filling.
  4. Top with second crust and seal edges with a fork.
  5. Cut small vents in top crust for steam escape.
  6. Mix egg with water and brush over crust.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes until crust is golden.
  8. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
Chicken Nuggets
⏱️ Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 12 minutes | 👥 Serves: 4
📝 Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken breast
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil for frying
  • Dipping sauce of choice
👨‍🍳 Instructions:
  1. Cut chicken breasts into nugget-sized pieces.
  2. Set up three bowls: flour, beaten eggs, breadcrumbs.
  3. Coat each nugget in flour, then egg, then breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Fry nuggets 3-4 minutes per side until golden.
  6. Drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
